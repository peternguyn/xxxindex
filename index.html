DIONYSUS

PRODUCT DETAILS

Defining handbags, shoulder bags and precious mini bags, the tiger-head hardware is an ode to the Greek god Dionysus, who in myth is said to have crossed the river Tigris on a tiger sent to him by Zeus. Expressed through Alessandro Michele's creativity, the Beloved lines connect the House tradition with contemporary details.

Cotton knit polo jumper with Web

PRODUCT DETAILS.

Style ‎653624 XKBUR 9182 Codes that have become synonymous with the House remain a source of inspiration for the Creative Director. Amongst the most instantly recognizable is the Web stripe—originally conceived as an interpretation of the straps used to hold horseback saddles in place. Here, it is presented as a trim upon a cotton knit polo.

Viscose linen single-breasted jacket

PRODUCT DETAILS

Style ‎652009 ZAFHJ 2236 Refined, yet unexpected details adorn new expressions of structure. From jackets that play with new proportions to trousers that redefine fit, codes that have become synonymous with the House remain a symbol of lasting beauty. Presented in clay red, the single-breasted jacket is renewed with a viscose linen blend, giving it a fresher feel.

Baby cotton gift set with Interlocking G

PRODUCT DETAILS

Style ‎662925 XJDM4 4306 The Interlocking G has remained one of the most symbolic codes of the House since its introduction. Here, the emblem is paired with the Web stripe atop a sleepsuit and bib for baby. A matching hat completes the gift set.

The Virgin Violet, Violet scented lip

PRODUCT DETAILS

Style ‎548812 99999 0099 Gucci's first Haute Perfumery line, The Alchemist's Garden unfolds within an imagined laboratory filled with curious jars and mesmerizing scents. Blended by master perfumer Alberto Morillas under Alessandro Michele's creative direction, the collection, made up of eaux de parfum, perfumed oils, acque profumate ("scented waters") and a candle, is inspired by the art of alchemy and fragrance-making. The candle was blended to accompany the wearable scents and is presented in a porcelain jar to be displayed and enjoyed at home.

Double G ice cream cone earring

PRODUCT DETAILS

Style ‎661187 J1D50 8031 Storybook-inspired illustrations continue to be seen throughout Gucci’s most recent collections. For Ouverture, they take the shape of farm animals, fruits and vegetables inviting the wearer to view the world from an imaginative perspective. Speaking to this playful narrative, this single earring is shaped as an ice cream cone with sparkling crystal accents.
